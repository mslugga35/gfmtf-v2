## About GFM Training Academy
GFM Training Academy is an elite baseball and softball training facility in Sanford, Florida. Founded by Coach Larry Grayson, a 2x MLB Draft pick and NJCAA First Team All-American, we specialize in developing youth athletes from ages 8-18.

## Key Differentiators
- **Homeschool Athlete Focus**: One of the few facilities offering daytime training for homeschool students, Florida Virtual School athletes, and flexible scheduling for non-traditional students.
- **Professional Experience**: Coach Larry Grayson played 5 seasons in the Texas Rangers minor league organization, reaching AA level.
- **Complete Development**: We train hitting, pitching, fielding, speed/agility, arm care, and the mental game.
- **PE Credit Documentation**: Available for homeschool athletes who need verified physical education hours.

## Our Programs
1. **Homeschool Athlete Training** - Daytime sessions designed for homeschool and FLVS students
2. **1-on-1 Private Training** - Individual lessons with video analysis
3. **Group & Team Training** - Small group sessions for travel ball teams
4. **Hitting & Batting Lessons** - Swing mechanics and bat speed development
5. **Pitching Development** - Velocity, mechanics, and pitch arsenal training
6. **Arm Care Program** - Injury prevention and arm health
7. **Speed & Agility Training** - 60-yard dash improvement, baserunning
8. **Fielding Fundamentals** - Infield and outfield defensive training

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What age groups do you train?
We train athletes from ages 8-18, from youth travel ball to high school varsity level.

### Do you offer training for homeschool athletes?
Yes! We specialize in daytime training for homeschool students, Florida Virtual School (FLVS) students, and non-traditional schedule athletes. We can provide PE credit documentation.

### What sports do you train?
We train both baseball and softball athletes in hitting, pitching, fielding, speed/agility, and arm care.

### Where is GFM Training Academy located?
We are based in Sanford, FL, serving all of Seminole County and Central Florida.

### What makes your training different?
Coach Larry Grayson brings 5 years of professional minor league experience and was a 2x MLB Draft pick. We focus on complete player development, not just one skill.

### Do you offer team training?
Yes, we offer small group and full team training sessions, perfect for travel ball teams looking to improve together.
